## RECORDING NOTES

1. **Screen record at 1080p minimum.** Clean desktop — close tabs, hide bookmarks bar.
2. **No background noise.** Record in quiet room. Use a decent mic if possible.
3. **Each scene is one screen recording.** No fancy edits needed — just cut between scenes.
4. **Energy matters more than polish.** Sound excited about what you built. You should sound like you genuinely enjoy this.
5. **Don't read the script word-for-word.** Know the beats, speak naturally. The script is a guide.
6. **If camera:** Good lighting on face. Look at the lens, not the screen.
7. **If no camera:** Record terminal/IDE/code as "visual proof" while you talk over it.
