RECORDING & COMPRESSION
============================================================

RECORDING SETTINGS (to get under 20 MB):
  - Resolution: 720p (1280x720), NOT 1080p or 4K
  - Frame rate: 24 or 30 fps
  - Use your phone's front camera, NOT screen recording software
  - Most phones: Settings > Camera > Record Video > 720p HD

AFTER RECORDING — COMPRESS:
  Option A (easiest): Upload to https://www.freeconvert.com/video-compressor
      Target: under 20 MB, resolution 720p
  Option B: Use HandBrake (free, desktop)
      Preset: "Very Fast 720p30", quality RF 28-30
  Option C: Send via WhatsApp to yourself first
      WhatsApp auto-compresses — then save the compressed version

RECORDING TIPS:
  - Phone at eye level, stable (lean against something)
  - Window/natural light facing you, not behind you
  - Quiet room
  - Don't read word-for-word — know the flow, speak naturally
  - Energy > perfection
  - If you stumble, pause and restart that section
